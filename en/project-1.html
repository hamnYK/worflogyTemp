Core Features

Issue-based Workflow Ontology

AI Policy Assetization
Convert complex business rules and decision-making criteria into explicit policy knowledge interpretable by artificial intelligence, to support intelligent automated decision-making.

Core Technology
Fractal Ontology-based Knowledge Graph Structuring & AI Communication Technology
Automatically convert Issue-Activity-Judgment flows into ontology triples and accumulate knowledge in self-similar (fractal) hierarchical structures. The RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline uses the accumulated graph as real-time context to deliver customized AI communication for the organization.

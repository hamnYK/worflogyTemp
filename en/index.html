Legacy vs Next-Gen

A Fractal Ontology System that Goes Beyond Legacy KMS

Depart from legacy folder classification systems where management costs only grow as data increases, and establish a dynamic knowledge system where information connects and evolves on its own.

Legacy System

Legacy KMS

A static classification system based on folders and tags. As data accumulates, contexts are lost, making it easy to devolve into a 'data graveyard'.

  • Static and fragmented folder/tag structure
  • Loss of core context over time
  • Exponential management costs as data accumulates
  • Limitations of simple search queries

Worflogy Fractal Ontology

Based on dynamic contexts where relationships between knowledge are defined self-actively. From the smallest unit of notes to the entire project, they share the same logic and expand infinitely.

  • Relationship-defining dynamic context structure
  • Perfect preservation of flow of thought and causal relationships
  • Organic scalability based on fractal structure
  • Advanced collaboration delivering strategic context to AI

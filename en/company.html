About Worflogy

Worflogy Inc. is a knowledge architecture R&D startup founded on the philosophy of "translating thoughts into context and communicating." Beyond being a mere solution provider, we pioneer a new paradigm of knowledge collaboration as a Bottom-Up Design Technology Partner that transforms the potential of individuals and organizations into valuable intellectual capital.
